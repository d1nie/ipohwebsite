IPOH

Ipoh, the capital of Perak in Malaysia, is a city rich in history, boasting colonial-era architecture
such as the iconic Ipoh Railway Station. Once a prominent center for tin mining, the city showcases its
past wealth through ornate shophouses and mansions. With a multicultural population, Ipoh offers vibrant
festivals, cave temples like Kek Lok Tong, and outdoor activities amidst lush rainforests and limestone hills.
